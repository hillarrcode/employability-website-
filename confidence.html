Self-Confidence

Self-confidence is the ability to act even when you are unsure how you will complete the task.

Ways of Building Self-Confidence

  • Self-acceptance
  • Positive talk
  • Good friends
  • Role models
  • Learning from the past
  • Grooming

Benefits

  • Find solutions to problems
  • Avoid unhealthy competition
  • Overcome peer pressure
  • Make new friends and interact confidently
  • Listen to others without offense
  • Boosts self-worth and joy in life

Hindrances

  • Self-doubt
  • Toxic people
  • Violence
  • Stereotyping
  • Radicalization
  • Gender discrimination
